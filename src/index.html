TRAVEL
By Plane
Both Lansdowne Resort and Clarion Inn are located approximately 20 minutes from Dulles Airport. Both hotels offer shuttle services to and from Dulles Airport. Rental car reservations can also be made for pick-up and drop-off at Dulles Airport should you prefer to drive upon arrival.
By Car
Approximate travel times by car to Leesburg, VA are as follows:
New York, NY - 4.5 hours
Norfolk, VA - 3.5-4 hours
Richmond, VA - 2-2.5 hours
Philadelphia, PA - 3 hours
Boston, MA - 8 hours
By Train
The closest train station is Union Station in Washington, DC. Because Leesburg, VA is located approximate 45 minutes to 1 hour west of Washington, DC, we recommend arranging transportation by car upon arrival.
